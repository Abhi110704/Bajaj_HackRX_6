How to Use

1. Describe a Scenario: The AI is trained to understand shorthand. Simply describe the key facts of a case, and the AI will interpret it as a claim scenario.

Good Example:

"46M, knee surgery, 3-month policy"

Another Good Example:

"Is a 46-year-old male with a 3-month-old policy covered for knee surgery?"

2. Interpret the Response: The AI will provide a direct "Yes" or "No" answer based *strictly* on the rules found in the uploaded documents. The explanation will highlight the most critical clause and the source document that determined the decision.

3. Test Different Scenarios: Try changing key details in your query to see how the outcome changes. For example, changing the policy age from "3 months" to "3 years" should result in a different answer based on the waiting period clauses in the sample documents.
